# Greetings! I'm Katherine Kimberling
## (But only when I'm in trouble. Feel free to call me Katie.)


### **About Me:**
**Data nerd. Educator. Entrepreneur. Artist. Outdoor enthusiast.**

<u>The short version</u>:

I have extensive experience in the arenas of public health (as a biostatistical epidemiologist), higher education (adjunct instructor at the college level and college STEM tutor), entrepreneurship (founder and owner of a small business), PR, and survey research (design, collection, analysis, presentation).

<u>If you want more details</u>:

My full time career of late has been that of entrepreneur and STEM educator - I built a college-level tutoring company from the ground up and have spent several years both growing/managing the business and working with clients in higher levels of math and statistics. I also provide executive functioning coaching and academic life coaching.

Prior to my current career I was the senior vice president and director of operations for a D.C.-based PR company (I supervised their Oklahoma City office), where I managed client accounts and oversaw the data collection, analysis and dissemination for both qualitative and quantitative projects.

Just before that, the University of Oklahoma gave me a budget and 1,300 square feet of empty space (in an elderly campus building) and tasked me with constructing, staffing, and maintaining a 20-station CATI center, "OU POLL" (Oklahoma Univerity Public Opinion Learning Laboratory).
